Our Roots

From humble beginnings, Zenith has experienced a meteoric rise and is now firmly established as a leader in the beauty and cosmetics industry.

Starting life 12 years ago as an independent high street hair salon, it's focus has always been on finding and exploiting niches by extensively researching the latest trends and continuously refreshing its products and services to cater to a diverse and ever-changing market.

In the years since it was founded, Zenith has continued to expand, providing new, innovative services using only the highest quality professional products, delivered by its expert and knowledgeable stylists, artists, and therapists. We don't settle for less, and neither should you.

Our Awards

We are proud to be consistently named the number one cosmetic services provider. We attribute this success to our consummate professionalism in maintaining high standards. We perfect our craft and stop at nothing to make sure our clients leave with a smile on their face.

Beauty is our passion, and we love what we do.
